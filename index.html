Full Job Description

Ohlmec is seeking experienced Boilermakers for ongoing FIFO shutdown works across the Pilbara Region commencing from 26/08.

  • Trade Certificate in Boilermaking
  • Valid Australian Driver's Licence
  • Working at Heights (current within 2 years) optional
  • Confined Space Entry (current within 2 years) optional
  • Basic Fire certificate
  • Construction OHS White Card
  • Australian Work Rights
  • FMG induction highly desirable
